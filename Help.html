Troubleshooting & Common Fixes

🔁 Reload Issues

If the site seems frozen, try refreshing the page. Clear your browser cache and reload if the issue persists.

🚫 Cannot Join Rooms

Ensure your internet connection is stable. Try switching networks or using a different browser.

🔐 Account Access

Use the same browser/device to reconnect anonymously. For registered accounts, ensure cookies are enabled.

🔊 No Sound?

Check your volume settings and ensure browser permissions are granted for media access.
